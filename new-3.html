Food

For newcomers in the UAE, managing food costs amidst a high cost of living can be challenging, but several strategies make it easier to keep expenses under control. Shopping smart is essential: local supermarkets like Carrefour, Lulu Hypermarket, and Union Coop offer frequent promotions on staples, especially if you buy in bulk. Choosing regional brands instead of imported products can significantly reduce your grocery bills without sacrificing quality. Fresh fruits and vegetables are also often more affordable at local outdoor markets, which offer seasonal produce at competitive prices. Dining out can be pricey, so opting for traditional Emirati or Middle Eastern food spots, like shawarma or falafel shops, is a budget-friendly way to enjoy local cuisine. Cooking at home is usually cheaper and lets you experiment with local ingredients, while meal-prepping can help reduce food waste and make weekday meals convenient and affordable. Many supermarkets offer discount sections with near-expiry items, which are ideal for immediate use.
